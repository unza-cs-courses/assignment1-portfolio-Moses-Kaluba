About Me

I'm a passionate web developer with a strong foundation in creating responsive, user-friendly websites and web applications. My journey into web development began when I built my first website in high school, and I've been captivated by the endless possibilities of the web ever since.

I recently completed my studies in Web Programming and Technologies at University of Zambia, where I gained hands-on experience with modern web development practices. What excites me most about web development is the ability to create solutions that can reach and impact people across the globe. I'm particularly interested in front-end development, where I can combine technical skills with creative design to build intuitive user experiences.

My approach to development focuses on writing clean, maintainable code while ensuring accessibility and performance are never compromised. I believe that great websites should work for everyone, regardless of their device or abilities. This commitment to inclusive design drives me to stay updated with the latest web standards and best practices.
